## How to use these numbers

- State them matter-of-factly, never as a flex
- Pair success numbers with struggle numbers for contrast
- Use specific figures ($1.2M, 20%, 7x) — never round to "millions" or "a lot"
- Always tie the number to a lesson, not just a brag
