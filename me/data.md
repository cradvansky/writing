## What NOT to do with data

- **Never invent a stat.** If the topic needs a number Chris hasn't cited, ask him or leave it out.
- **Never round up.** If he said "$8/mo or so", don't write "less than $10/mo." Use his words.
- **Never put a competitor's stat in his mouth.** Even if it would land the point.
- **Never fabricate revenue / MRR / ARR numbers.** Not in any sample. If a draft needs one, ask first.
- **Never overstate the team.** It's solo. Not "we", not "our team" in marketing copy unless explicitly working with a collaborator and they're tagged.
