# Stories & Anecdotes

> The stories you tell repeatedly — origin stories, proof points, client wins, failures. These are your most powerful content assets. The AI needs the actual story, not a summary.

## How to add a story

For each story, include:
1. **The story itself** — paste the actual words you'd say, as close to verbatim as possible
2. **Why you tell it** — what lesson or point it supports
3. **When to use it** — which topics or contexts trigger this story
